Dumplings recipe

Ingridients

dough

  • flour
  • egg
  • water
  • salt

filling

  • beef
  • butter
  • parsley
  • onion
  • garlic
  • salt and other seasonings you prefer

preparation

  • water
  • salt
  • butter

Directions

  1. Mix flour, egg and salt, add some water and start kneding tight dough. Kneed until the dough is consistent and elastic. Wrap it in plastic wrap and leave at room temperature for 20 minets.
  2. Mix thoroughly the ingridients for the filling.
  3. Roll the dough and cut small circles, stuff them with the filling and shape them any way you prefer.
  4. After all the dough is stuffed, boil walet in a big pot with a teaspoon of salt. after the water is boiled put the dumplings in water and boil them for approximetly 10-12 minets.
  5. Serve them in a plate with a peace of butter so that they dont stick together and enjoy)))
